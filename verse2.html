THE DAY OF RESURRECTION


(C) VERSE 2
As the last footsteps fades Then
the two angles are descended,
Violently coming to rade MUNKAR
AND NAKIR,the harsh and the fair
Who will question your soul laid
bare Who is you Lord? What path
do you take? What Seeds do you
sow for your soul's own sake Here
every single deeds are recorded
Even from your minor mistakes But
the truthful respond will bring life
to light And the wrong doers will
tremble in fear due to that night
Here every Soul's destiny decided
right here.
