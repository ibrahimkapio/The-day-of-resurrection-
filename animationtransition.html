About Us

A Reminder for the Heart, A Call for the Soul
Welcome to Young Muslim Reminder
A platform dedicated to reviving the awareness of the Day of Resurrection (Yawm al-Qiyamah)
and strengthening our connection with Allah. In a world filled with distractions, it’s easy to lose sight
of the ultimate truth—the moment when we will stand before our Creator.Our mission is to provide
thought-provoking reminders, Quranic verses, Hadith, and reflections that awaken the heart and inspire righteous actions. .

Our Services

To remind Mankind About the Day of Judgement.

• Daily & Weekly Reminders – Short yet impactful messages to keep the Hereafter in focus.
• Quran & Hadith Reflections – Wisdom from the words of Allah and the teachings of Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ).
• Inspiration for Young Muslims – Practical advice on living a mindful, faith-driven life. • Community & Engagement – A space to connect, learn, and grow in faith together.

Our Purpose

We believe that every heartbeat brings us closer to the Hereafter.
Our goal is to remind, uplift, and encourage young Muslims to live with purpose,
sincerity, and preparation for the ultimate meeting with Allah. May our reminders serve as a means of guidance,
and may Allah grant us all success in this life and the next. Follow us, reflect, and prepare – because the Day of Resurrection is near.
